INTERNSHIPS

A number of people volunteer their time and effort to keep the displays in good order and ready for visitors to come and enjoy.

As a volunteer you will:

  • Get to see behind the scenes of a working museum
  • Have access to staff-only lectures
  • Volunteers and staff join a monthly lunch together to discuss ideas for future exhibits and strategies for the museum.
Please contact us if you’d like to find out more about how you can get involved.
