About us

Community Science Museum is a sci-museum for young and old, made for exploring together.

The aim of our museum is to create a space where everyone can experience the wonders of our discoveries and perhaps even ignite a lifelong passion to continue the exploration of the world around us.

We believe science should not be confined to the textbook, but brought to live through exhibits.

This is why we have over 1000 different exhibits on the many varied subjects of science to explore.

Many of these exhibits are designed for you to interact with and play around to see science come to life (apart from the evolution exhibits – where the dinosaurs only come to life at night when everyone’s gone home)!
